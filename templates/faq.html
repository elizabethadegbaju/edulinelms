FAQ

“Reading is important, because if you can read, you can learn anything about everything and everything about anything.” —Tomie dePaola

For how long can I reserve a book before my reservation is cancelled?

When a book is reserved, it must be picked up from the library the same day or the following day.

Your reservation will be cleared if it is not picked up before the end of the following day.

What happens if I fail to return a book on time?

A fine to the tune of ₦500 will be charged to your account once the stated date is passed.

And for every consequent day, you will be charge an additional ₦100 until the book is returned.

Can I renew a borrowed book online?

No, you cannot. You Are advised to visit the library with the book to extend the existing return date before it is reached.

Once the return date has passed, you cannot extend the return date.

How long can I keep a book without being fined?

You are allowed to borrow a book for maximum of 10 days after which you will be fined. The book can be returned anytime within those 10 days without any charges to your account.

10 days is from the date of pick up from the library.
